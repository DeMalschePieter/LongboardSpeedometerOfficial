Welcome!

This is the longboard speedometer website, powered by the raspberry pi 3 under the longboard. On this website you start a new sessions, simply by clicking the 'add session' menu button. A new seesion will start and by refreshing the page you will see real time values like elapsed time, distance, maxium speed. Closing the session is even more easy, just press the 'stop session' button and your session will be uploaded t0 the sessions page.
